Machine Learning/Data Mining Internship

September 6​th​ 2018

Internship Overview

EyeCue Lab​ (Portland, Oregon) is offering a paid internship for candidates who have experience in prototyping and building machine learning/data mining solutions. In this internship, you will complete a general analysis on how to determine similarity between different records in selected data sets.

For example, you will determine how similar restaurants are within a city based on cuisine type, location, price range, etc. Your approach to the problem should be extensible so that it canalso be applied to different data sets, such as exploring similarities in movies released in the last 20 years based on actors, genre, directors, keywords, etc.

Goals

  1. 1. Create the initial proof-of-concept algorithm that provides similarity scores using data provided.
  2. 2. Work with development team to integrate solution into existing product.

Additional Internship Information

The internship is paid at $20/hr and the minimum requirement is 20 hours a week. The projectis managed using agile methodology; participation in daily stand-up/check-in and a minimum of 4 weekly sprints are required. Data can be provided in MySQL database or CSV format.

Your solution can be drafted in any scripting language that you are familiar with (e.g. Python,C++, R, etc). The internship is expected to last between 4 and 8 weeks, starting in October 2018. Interns may work on-site or remotely. Laptop is provided for on-site candidates only.

Qualifications

  • PhD or Master’s with at least a year of related working experience in applied mathematics, data/information science, computer science or statistical analysis fields.
  • Demonstrated experience in machine learning or data mining analysis
  • Familiarity with programming languages such as Python, C++, Java, or other scripting languages.
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills.
